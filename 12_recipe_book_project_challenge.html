Spicy Grilled Chicken Wrap

Ingredients:

Ingredient Quantity
Grilled Chicken Breast 1 piece
Whole Wheat Tortilla 1 wrap
Fresh Lettuce 1 cup
Sliced Tomatoes 1/2 cup
Red Onion 1/4 cup
Spicy Mayo Sauce 2 tbsp
Shredded Cheese 1/4 cup

Details:

  • Servings: 1
  • Prep Time: 10 minutes
  • Cook Time: 10 minutes

Steps:

  1. Grill the chicken breast until fully cooked and juicy.
  2. Warm the whole wheat tortilla in a pan for 1-2 minutes.
  3. Spread spicy mayo sauce evenly on the tortilla.
  4. Layer fresh lettuce, sliced tomatoes, red onion, and shredded cheese on the tortilla.
  5. Place the grilled chicken on top of the ingredients.
  6. Roll the tortilla into a wrap and cut in half before serving.

Creamy Pesto Pasta

Ingredients:

Ingredient Quantity
Fettuccine Pasta 200g
Creamy Pesto Sauce 1/2 cup
Grilled Mushrooms 1/2 cup
Cherry Tomatoes 1/4 cup
Fresh Spinach 1 cup
Grated Parmesan Cheese 1/4 cup

Details:

  • Servings: 2
  • Prep Time: 10 minutes
  • Cook Time: 15 minutes

Steps:

  1. Boil the fettuccine pasta in salted water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat a pan and saut√© the grilled mushrooms and cherry tomatoes.
  3. Add fresh spinach to the pan and cook until wilted.
  4. Stir in the creamy pesto sauce and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  5. Combine the cooked fettuccine with the sauce and vegetables.
  6. Sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese on top before serving.

Mediterranean Veggie Bowl

Ingredients:

Ingredient Quantity
Quinoa or Couscous 1 cup (cooked)
Roasted Zucchini 1/2 cup
Roasted Eggplant 1/2 cup
Hummus 2 tbsp
Feta Cheese 1/4 cup
Olives 1/4 cup
Cucumber Slices 1/2 cup
Lemon-Tahini Dressing 2 tbsp
Details:

Details:

  • Servings: 2
  • Prep Time: 15 minutes
  • Cook Time: 10 minutes

Steps:

  1. Cook quinoa or couscous according to package instructions and set aside.
  2. Roast zucchini and eggplant in the oven until tender and lightly browned.
  3. In a bowl, arrange the cooked quinoa, roasted vegetables, and cucumber slices.
  4. Add hummus, feta cheese, and olives on top of the bowl.
  5. Drizzle with lemon-tahini dressing before serving.
